Our Mission

Web Developer Spaceship mission: " Make traveling in to space affordable for Simpson students."

Here at NASEMOON, we believe that allowing college students to explore space on their own creates a much more interactive learning environment, especially for those involved in the aerospace and engineering fields of major study.

Through our numerous offered scholarships, we can help students enjoy an excursion to the moon, which was seemingly impossible to do not very long ago.

To qualify for the trip, you must be:

  • A resident of Iowa
  • A Simpson College student at the time of the trip
  • Payed in full or on a payment plan

This trip to space is meant for educational purposes that are only provided in correspondence with Simpson College.
